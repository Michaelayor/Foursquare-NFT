How It Works

Create and sell your NFTs

  • Set up your wallet

    Once you’ve set up your wallet of choice, connect it to OpenSea by clicking the NFT Marketplace in the top right corner. Learn about the wallets we support.

  • Create your collection

    Click Create and set up your collection. Add social links, a description, profile & banner images, and set a secondary sales fee.

  • Add your NFTs

    Upload your work (image, video, audio, or 3D art), add a title and description, and customize your NFTs with properties, stats, and unlockable content.

  • List them for sale

    Choose between auctions, fixed-price listings, and declining-price listings. You choose how you want to sell your NFTs!

Q&A

We are a huge marketplace dedicated to connecting great artists of all FoursqaureNFT with their fans and unique token collectors!

  • Four Square NFTs operates by allowing artists to create unique digital artworks or collectibles, which can be tokenized as NFTs on the platform. These NFTs are then listed for sale or auction, where buyers can purchase them securely. Ownership transfers are recorded on the blockchain, enabling users to trade, sell, or showcase their digital assets within the marketplace.

  • While having a designer can enhance the quality of your digital assets, it is not a requirement to use Four Square NFTs. Artists can create their own unique digital artworks without the need for a designer to tokenize and list them as NFTs on the platform.

  • To start selling on Four Square NFTs, you'll need to create unique digital artworks or collectibles that you wish to tokenize as NFTs. You'll also need to set up an account on the platform to list your NFTs for sale or auction. Ensuring proper digital asset creation and understanding the tokenization process will be key to initiating sales on Four Square NFTs.

  • Upon receiving an order on Four Square NFTs, you will be notified of the purchase through the platform. After confirming the order details, ensuring payment verification, and transferring ownership securely to the buyer, you will receive the payment for the sale. Delivery of any associated files or content related to the NFT may be required, followed by potential feedback and ratings from buyers and sellers.
